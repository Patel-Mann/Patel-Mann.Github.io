The issue I was facing while going on hikes with a group of friends was that it was extremely difficult to communicate in areas with no signal. There was also no easy way to share small but important moments. For example, if I spotted poison ivy, it would be much easier to send a photo rather than use a walkie-talkie and try to describe the exact palce to watch out for.

My idea is to create a node-to-node communication device using an STM32 microcontroller and a 2.4 GHz NRF module, allowing users to exchange messages and images in areas without cellular connectivity.
