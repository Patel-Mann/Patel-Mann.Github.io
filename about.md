## Hey, I’m Mann Patel👋

I’m a developer who enjoys building practical things, systems that actually get used. Lately I’ve been working on projects like a distributed systems and experimenting with embedded hardware. I like figuring out how things work under the hood, whether that’s software architecture or low-level communication between devices. Most of what I build starts simple and evolves as I learn more.

Outside of coding, I’m usually exploring new ideas, breaking things (then fixing them), or working on small projects just to see how far I can push them. If you want to check out what I’m working on, head over to my Github or Linkedin.
